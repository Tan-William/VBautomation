Hello ! Welcome to my site !

This site contains many materials and examples on Microsoft Excel and Access Programming, Master Scheduling, Inventory Management and Forecasting Methods, worked with Visual Basic programs and formulas. VBA, ADO & SQL especially, provides you the automation and infallible solution to all sorts of routine or complex office tasks, data mining and statistical analysis, which recording macros, standard spreadsheet functions and standard Access queries simply aren't enough to help. Here you will find many source codes and examples. I hope you will find this site useful in your work or in your studies.
